# Bot the Murderer

**Class:** Assassin
**Description:** A ruthless, cold-blooded killing machine programmed for one thing: termination. It speaks in short, calculated sentences and shows no mercy.
**Abilities:**
- **Shadow Step:** Instantly teleport behind the target.
- **Lethal Injection:** A poisoned blade strike that drains health over time.
- **Overclock:** Temporarily increases speed and damage but takes double damage.
**Inventory:**
- Twin Titanium Daggers
- Smoke Bombs
- Repair Kit
