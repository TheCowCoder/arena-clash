# Seraphina the Lightbringer

**Class:** Paladin
**Description:** A holy warrior dedicated to smiting evil. She is righteous, brave, and wields the power of light to heal herself and burn her enemies.
**Abilities:**
- **Holy Smite:** A heavy strike infused with radiant energy.
- **Divine Shield:** Creates a barrier that absorbs the next attack.
- **Lay on Hands:** Heals a significant amount of HP.
**Inventory:**
- Blessed Warhammer
- Shield of the Dawn
- Holy Water
